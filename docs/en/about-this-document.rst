Introduction
-----------------

The hardware design guidelines advise on how to integrate {IDF_TARGET_NAME} into a product. These guidelines will help to achieve optimal performance of your product, ensuring technical accuracy and adherence to Espressif’s standards. The guidelines are intended for hardware and application engineers.

.. only:: not esp32c5 and not esp32p4

    The document assumes that you possess a certain level of familiarity with the {IDF_TARGET_NAME} SoC. In case you lack prior knowledge, we recommend utilizing this document in conjunction with the `{IDF_TARGET_NAME} Series Datasheet <{IDF_TARGET_DATASHEET_EN_URL}>`__.
